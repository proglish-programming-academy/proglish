About us

Hi, we are the team of Proglish Programming academy. We aim to teach programming and English language for free and easy. Why are these skills so important? Because they are the key to our future. Our goal is to help everyone to become fluent in English and programming so that they can earn money from it. Choose us because our teachers are professionals who have 3 years of experience in these fields and certifications from Quincy Larson. We are sure that you will be satisfied with our courses!
